Trackarr Federation

High-fidelity mockups for letting independent Trackarr instances link up and share data — opt-in, owner-controlled, allow-list only. Built to match the live design system exactly.

The owner console: master enable toggle, this instance's verifiable identity, default sharing scopes (catalogue · social · accounts · swarm), KPIs, and the partner allow-list with handshake flow. Includes the inbound-request approval modal — click Review on the pending request.
